.. _login:

=======
Sign in
=======

.. toctree::

To Sign in on platform:

1. Open /login page (you will see log in form with two inputs. First for email address and second for password)

2. Enter you email and password and click on "Sign in" button

Additionally you can "Remember" the email you've entered for next signs in on platform, to do this set checkbox on "Remember me" text.

If you forgot password and want to reset it, click on "Forgot password?" link and follow the instructions.
