============
Registration
============

.. toctree::

To register user on platform you have to open website and follow the instructions below:

1. Click on "Create account" link (registration form will be opened)

2. Select account type you want to create while register user

.. warning:: **Platform supports two types of account, they are personal and company. Personal account fit to small companies who want to use a couple of mailboxes. Company account better fit to big companies who contains big amount of employees**

3. Fill in required details and proceed according to instructions. :ref:`Detailed instructions. <registration-instruction>`

On platform exist password policy which you can read :ref:`here <password-policy>`.
